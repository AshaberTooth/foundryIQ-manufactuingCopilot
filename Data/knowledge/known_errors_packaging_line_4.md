# Known Errors - Packaging Line 4

## Repeated E-217 after restart

Repeated E-217 alarms after restart are commonly caused by vibration of the photo-eye sensor bracket.

Symptoms:

- Conveyor starts normally.
- Conveyor stops within 30 to 90 seconds.
- Motor temperature remains normal.
- No actual downstream obstruction is found.

Resolution:

- Realign photo-eye sensor.
- Tighten or replace sensor mounting bracket.
- Confirm signal stability for 10 minutes after restart.
