# Packaging Line 4 Conveyor OEM Manual

## Troubleshooting: Repeated stop after restart

If the conveyor repeatedly stops shortly after restart:

1. Check for downstream obstruction.
2. Check photo-eye sensor alignment.
3. Check sensor bracket vibration.
4. Check belt tension.
5. Check motor overload relay.
6. Review recent maintenance actions.

For alarm E-217, inspect sensor signal stability before replacing motor components.
