Rank Likely Cause Why It Matters

Safe First Checks

  1. Perform lockout/tagout on Packaging Line 4 Conveyor before any physical inspection.
  2. Verify zero-energy state before touching the sensor, bracket, belt, or conveyor components.
  3. Inspect the photo-eye sensor alignment and confirm it is pointed correctly at the target.
  4. Check mounting bracket vibration, loose fasteners, damage, or mechanical looseness.
  5. Inspect the conveyor path for obstruction and verify belt tension after LOTO.
  6. Restart only after safety checks are complete and monitor signal stability for at least 10 minutes.

Recommended Corrective Action

  • Realign the photo-eye sensor after completing lockout/tagout.
  • Tighten or replace the photo-eye sensor mounting bracket if vibration or looseness is found.
  • If signal instability continues, replace the photo-eye sensor unit.
  • Perform a functional restart test and confirm alarm E-217 no longer triggers.
  • Document the action in the draft maintenance work order and route it for supervisor review.
